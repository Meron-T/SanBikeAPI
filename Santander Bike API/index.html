How it works

Getting started is easy.Simply hire a bike, ride it wherever you like, then return it to a docking station.
The video on the right outlines how this is done.

What you pay

Santander cycles has two hiring options:

Daily

It costs £2 to access a bike for 24 hours. The first 30 minutes of each journey is free - after that it costs £2 per 30 minutes.

Yearly

If you become a member, you can use the bikes all year round for £90. Sign up for annual membership.
